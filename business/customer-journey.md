# Customer Journey Map for Transaction Tracker

## Phase 1: Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Financial service providers experience frequent transaction delays or issues.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of visibility into transaction status; manual troubleshooting processes are time-consuming.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, anxiety about customer satisfaction, urgency to resolve issues.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide educational content on the importance of transaction monitoring; showcase success stories of reduced downtime.
- **Metric**: Number of leads generated through awareness campaigns (e.g., website visits, content downloads).

## Phase 2: Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Providers actively seek solutions to improve transaction monitoring and issue resolution.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelmed by options; uncertainty about ROI and effectiveness of solutions.
- **User Emotions**: Confusion, cautious optimism, desire for reassurance.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer personalized demos and case studies; provide comparison charts against competitors.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests or inquiries received.

## Phase 3: Try
- **Trigger Event**: Providers decide to trial the transaction-tracker platform.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup complexity; integration challenges with existing systems.
- **User Emotions**: Hesitation, hopefulness, stress during implementation.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide step-by-step onboarding support; offer a dedicated customer success manager during the trial.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate (percentage of trials that convert to paid subscriptions).

## Phase 4: Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Providers successfully implement the transaction-tracker platform.
- **Friction Points**: Learning curve for staff; potential resistance to change from existing processes.
- **User Emotions**: Relief, satisfaction, but also apprehension about ongoing usage.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Continuous training sessions; proactive check-ins to ensure satisfaction and address concerns.
- **Metric**: User engagement metrics (e.g., daily active users, feature usage rates).

## Phase 5: Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Providers see positive results and consider additional features or scaling usage.
- **Friction Points**: Budget constraints; uncertainty about which additional features to adopt.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement about potential growth, but also concern about costs and resource allocation.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer tailored packages for expansion; provide insights on usage data to demonstrate value.
- **Metric**: Revenue growth from upsells or additional feature adoption.
