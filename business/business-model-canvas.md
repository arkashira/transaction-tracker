# Business Model Canvas for Transaction Tracker

## Customer Segments
- Financial service providers (e.g., banks, credit unions)
- Transaction processing companies (e.g., payment gateways, fintech firms)
- E-commerce platforms requiring transaction oversight
- Merchants and retailers with high transaction volumes

## Value Propositions
- Real-time transaction monitoring to quickly identify and resolve issues
- Automated troubleshooting to reduce downtime and manual intervention
- Enhanced customer satisfaction through timely updates and resolutions
- Compliance support for regulatory requirements in transaction processing

## Channels
- Direct sales through a dedicated sales team targeting financial institutions
- Online marketing and lead generation via the product website
- Partnerships with fintech solution providers for bundled offerings
- Industry conferences and trade shows for product demonstrations

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated account management for enterprise clients
- Online support portal with FAQs, troubleshooting guides, and live chat
- Regular webinars and training sessions for users
- Feedback loops for continuous improvement based on customer input

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription-based pricing model: 
  - Basic Plan: $500/month (17,500 THB)
  - Professional Plan: $1,000/month (35,000 THB)
  - Enterprise Plan: $2,500/month (87,500 THB)
- Transaction fees for high-volume users (e.g., $0.10 per transaction)
- Custom integration and consulting services billed hourly

## Key Resources
- Development team for ongoing product enhancements and support
- Cloud infrastructure for hosting and data processing
- Customer support team for handling inquiries and troubleshooting
- Marketing and sales team for customer acquisition and retention

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and improvement of the transaction monitoring platform
- Marketing campaigns to raise awareness and generate leads
- Customer support and training to ensure user satisfaction
- Data analysis to identify trends and improve product features

## Key Partners
- Cloud service providers for hosting solutions (e.g., AWS, Azure)
- Payment processors and gateways for integration partnerships
- Regulatory bodies for compliance guidance and updates
- Industry associations for networking and market insights

## Cost Structure
- Salaries for development, sales, and support teams
- Cloud hosting and infrastructure costs (estimated at $2,000/month or 70,000 THB)
- Marketing and advertising expenses (estimated at $1,000/month or 35,000 THB)
- Customer support tools and software subscriptions (estimated at $500/month or 17,500 THB)
